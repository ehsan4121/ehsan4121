**What's happening?**
- **Current Situation**: Today's data protection (encryption) assumes regular computers can't crack it
- **Future Threat**: Quantum computers (much faster than today's computers) might break current encryption

**Imagine this**: Like someone stealing a locked safe today, knowing they'll get better tools to open it in 5 years!

## **üìã The 3-Step Plan to Become Quantum Safe**

### **Step 1: DISCOVER - "Find Your Locks"**
**What to do**: Find ALL encryption in your systems
- **Look at**: Source code, applications, databases
- **Create**: CBOM (Cryptography Bill of Materials) - a complete list
- **Like**: Making an inventory of every lock and key in your organization

### **Step 2: OBSERVE - "Watch Your Locks Work"**
**What to do**: See encryption in action
- **Combine**: Static (code) + Dynamic (running systems) views
- **Watch**: How encryption keys are exchanged (like in websites with "https")
- **Prioritize**: Which systems need fixing first based on risk

### **Step 3: TRANSFORM - "Upgrade Your Locks"**
**What to do**: Replace old encryption with quantum-safe versions
- **Implement**: New quantum-safe algorithms
- **Use**: Crypto-agility (easy switching between encryption methods)
- **Make it Easy**: Use API calls instead of rebuilding everything
